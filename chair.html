CHAIRS

As an object of design, a chair is unsurpassed in the challenges it sets an industrial designer. In its purest form, a chair is simultaneously comfortable, durable, ergonomically responsible and an object of beauty.Our range of chairs embodies these ideals, yet offers a wide range of styles to suit different nuances in taste and function. Our dining chairs, lounge chairs, occasional chairs and office chairs range from contemporary chairs in a wide selection of material to understated, classic modern chairs with the recognizable Kandy touch.
